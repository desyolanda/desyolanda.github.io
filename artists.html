    ITERATION - 2 →

    CREATE A STYLE GUIDE AND IMPLEMENT IT INTO THE DESIGN.
    THEN, TEST IF THE DESIGN FOLLOWS THE PREDEFINED DESIGN PRINCIPLES BY CONDUCTING HEURISTICS TESTING.

    BY ADDING COLORS AND STYLE, THE UI ELEMENTS ARE EASIER TO RECOGNIZE. FURTHERMORE, THE CONSISTENCY OF STYLE BETWEEN THE SCREENS CREATES A COHESIVE DESIGN.

    HEURISTICS TESTING IS CONDUCTED BY FOLLOWING JAKOB NIELSEN'S TEN GENERAL PRINCIPLES OF INTERACTION DESIGN.
