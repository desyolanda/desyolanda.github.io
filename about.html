A LITTLE BIT ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Desy Yolanda, a Visual Designer, and I'm passionate about solving problems through design. I've always believed the most impactful and beautiful designs are created with a passion for understanding human needs that are fulfilled by creative output. It's a belief that has made me a designer.

I am a restaurant owner turned designer. My experience in the service industry and owning a business have taught me about human understanding, and how certain changes that I made based on feedback can affect my customers' behaviors. I believe that my experience is helping me to create great designs that not only meet clients' needs but also can give satisfaction to clients' customers.
