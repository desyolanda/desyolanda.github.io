ANALYSIS →

STRENGTHS

The first one in the industry that provides full service home building experience.

A great concept accompanied by technology which makes bulding home much easier.

It claims to be the fast and cheaper way of building homes.

Its remote service can reach clients from anywhere in the world.

WEAKNESSES

The company is new and does not have years of experience unlike its peers in the industry.

Full service home building might limit the clients involvment in the process.

OPPORTUNITIES

The company found its opportunities from the wildfires in California and had helped many people rebuild their homes.

They are grabbing the opportunity of building many homes in Texas to accomodate many who moved from California to Texas for cheaper living expenses and better job opportunities.

The demand of homes are increasing during the pandemic.

THREATS

Newer competitors with better technology and concept.

Housing demand is decreasing as the prices go up after the pandemic.
