WIRED MAGAZINE - October 2021

THE PAIN OF ALGORITHM
By Maia Szalavitz

"The latest weapon in the war on drugs is a predictive ai on your doctor’s computer. It can determine who receives treatment for pain—— and who doesn’t"

INTERACTIVE
COMPONENTS:

  • Bring movement the shapes on the background
  • Highlight the charts with color when hovered

  • Link the news about Katherine to its source
